DigiGon™

What is Digital Gondola?

Digital Gondola is a newer concept in in-store marketing. We have developed a sleek and attractive looking gondola for in-store promotions and stock display. It enables you to project an image, three-dimensional (3D), interactive, full-motion audio-visual files on the gondola through a computer display. Digigon will enhance in-store customer experience by developing Digital Gondolas for your brand display. As an early adopter of technological innovations, Digigon is committed to provide you a state of the art visual solution. We recommend the installation of Digital Gondola in supermarkets and surprise your customers.
